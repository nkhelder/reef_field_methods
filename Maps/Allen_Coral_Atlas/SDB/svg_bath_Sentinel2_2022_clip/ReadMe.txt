Description:
Bathymetry for Saint Vincent and the Grenadines clipped to the benthic habitat extent. Bathymetry was derived from Sentinel2 imagery collected from 2019-2022, following methodology outlined in this publication:

Li, J.; Knapp, D.E.;Lyons, M.; Roelfsema, C.; Phinn, S.;Schill, S.R.; Asner, G.P. Automated Global Shallow Water Bathymetryping Using Google Earth Engine.Remote Sens. 2021, 13, 1469. https://doi.org/10.3390/rs13081469
